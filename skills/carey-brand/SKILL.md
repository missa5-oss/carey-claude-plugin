# Carey Business School Brand Guidelines

## Brand Hierarchy

Johns Hopkins Carey Business School operates as a sub-brand under the Johns Hopkins University master brand. All materials must maintain alignment with both JHU and Carey-specific guidelines.

## Primary Colors

| Color | Hex Code | RGB | Usage |
|-------|----------|-----|-------|
| JHU Heritage Blue | #002D72 | 0, 45, 114 | Primary brand color, headers, titles |
| Carey Spirit Blue | #0072CE | 0, 114, 206 | Digital accent, links, highlights |

## Secondary Colors

| Color | Hex Code | RGB | Usage |
|-------|----------|-----|-------|
| Sable | #31261D | 49, 38, 29 | Text, formal documents |
| Warm Gray | #A59D95 | 165, 157, 149 | Backgrounds, borders |
| Sandstone | #CFB991 | 207, 185, 145 | Accents, highlights |

## Accent Colors

| Color | Hex Code | RGB | Usage |
|-------|----------|-----|-------|
| Orange | #CF4520 | 207, 69, 32 | CTAs, alerts, emphasis |
| Teal | #008080 | 0, 128, 128 | Data viz secondary |

## Typography

### Primary Typefaces
- **Gentona**: Headlines and titles (Bold weight)
- **Source Sans Pro**: Body text, captions, data

### Type Hierarchy
```
H1: Gentona Bold, 32pt, Heritage Blue
H2: Gentona Bold, 24pt, Heritage Blue
H3: Gentona Bold, 18pt, Heritage Blue
Body: Source Sans Pro Regular, 11pt, Sable
Caption: Source Sans Pro Regular, 9pt, Warm Gray
```

### Web-Safe Alternatives
When Gentona is unavailable:
- Headlines: Arial Black or Helvetica Bold
- Body: Arial or Helvetica

## Logo Usage

### Primary Logo
- Full-color preferred on white backgrounds
- White reverse on dark backgrounds
- Minimum width: 1.5 inches print / 150px digital

### Clear Space
- Minimum clear space = height of "J" in Johns Hopkins
- No other elements should intrude this space

### Prohibited Uses
- Do not stretch or distort
- Do not rotate
- Do not change colors
- Do not add effects (shadows, gradients)
- Do not place on busy backgrounds

## Data Visualization Standards

### Chart Color Sequence
1. Heritage Blue (#002D72) - Primary data
2. Spirit Blue (#0072CE) - Secondary data
3. Orange (#CF4520) - Tertiary/highlight
4. Teal (#008080) - Fourth series
5. Warm Gray (#A59D95) - Fifth series

### Chart Requirements
- White or light gray background
- Clear axis labels with units
- Data source citation
- Legend when multiple series
- Title describing the insight

### Accessibility
- Ensure 4.5:1 contrast ratio minimum
- Don't rely solely on color for meaning
- Use patterns/textures in addition to color
- Include alt text for digital charts

## Document Templates

### Reports
- Cover: Heritage Blue header band with logo
- Headers: Gentona Bold in Heritage Blue
- Body: Source Sans Pro, justified
- Page numbers: Bottom right
- Margins: 1 inch all sides

### Presentations
- 16:9 aspect ratio (widescreen)
- Title slides: Heritage Blue background, white text
- Content slides: White background
- Footer: Carey logo, slide number

### Executive Summaries
- Maximum 2 pages
- Key findings highlighted
- Action-oriented recommendations
- Visual summary of data

## File Naming Convention

```
YYYY-MM-DD_ProjectName_DocumentType_Version.ext

Examples:
2024-03-15_MBABrandStudy_Report_v1.docx
2024-03-15_MBABrandStudy_Presentation_Final.pptx
```

## Quality Checklist

Before finalizing any branded material:
- [ ] Correct logo version and placement
- [ ] Brand colors used (no off-brand colors)
- [ ] Approved fonts applied
- [ ] Proper clear space around logo
- [ ] Charts follow visualization standards
- [ ] Document follows template structure
- [ ] File named per convention
