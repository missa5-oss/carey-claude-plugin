# RFP Response Generation

## RFP Response Structure

### Standard Sections

1. **Cover Letter**
2. **Executive Summary**
3. **Understanding of Requirements**
4. **Proposed Methodology**
5. **Project Team**
6. **Timeline and Milestones**
7. **Budget**
8. **Relevant Experience**
9. **References**
10. **Appendices**

## Section Templates

### 1. Cover Letter Template

```markdown
[Date]

[Client Name]
[Title]
[Organization]
[Address]

RE: [RFP Title/Number]

Dear [Name]:

Carey Business School Market Research is pleased to submit this proposal in
response to your request for [brief description of project].

[1-2 sentences on why Carey is well-positioned for this work]

[1-2 sentences on key differentiators or relevant experience]

We welcome the opportunity to discuss this proposal and answer any questions.
Please contact [Name] at [email] or [phone].

Sincerely,

[Name]
[Title]
Carey Business School Market Research
```

## Budget Estimation Guidelines

### Personnel Rates (Internal Reference)

| Role | Hourly Rate Range |
|------|-------------------|
| Principal/Director | $200-300 |
| Senior Manager | $150-200 |
| Manager | $100-150 |
| Senior Analyst | $75-100 |
| Analyst | $50-75 |

### Data Collection Costs

| Method | Cost Range |
|--------|------------|
| Online survey (per complete) | $3-15 |
| Phone survey (per complete) | $25-75 |
| Focus group (per group) | $5,000-10,000 |
| IDI (per interview) | $200-500 |

### Respondent Incentives

| Audience | Survey | Focus Group | IDI |
|----------|--------|-------------|-----|
| General consumers | $5-25 | $75-125 | $50-100 |
| Business professionals | $25-75 | $150-250 | $100-200 |
| Executives | $75-150 | $300-500 | $250-400 |

## RFP Response Best Practices

### Do:
- Address every requirement in the RFP
- Use client's language and terminology
- Quantify experience and results
- Make it easy to evaluate (clear structure)
- Proofread thoroughly

### Don't:
- Use generic boilerplate
- Exceed page limits
- Miss the deadline
- Over-promise capabilities
- Ignore evaluation criteria
